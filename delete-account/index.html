1

3

Eliminación Final

Tus credenciales y datos serán purgados de Firebase de forma permanente en un plazo de 72 horas.

Account and Data Deletion

Account deletion request for the Prisma Noticias application, developed by Arandu Labs LLC.

Important Compliance Notice (Google Play)

To request the deletion of your Prisma Noticias account and all its associated data, please send us an email at soporte@arandulabs.dev or complete the form at the bottom of this page. Your authentication data will be deleted from our Firebase servers within a maximum of 72 hours.

Steps to request deletion:

1

Choose a Method

Send us a direct email or fill out the request form on this page indicating your account email.

2

Verification

We will process your request and verify the authentication data associated with your email.

3

Final Deletion

Your credentials and data will be permanently purged from Firebase within 72 hours.

Data Management, Treatment & Retention

Data Type Action / Status Retention Period
Authentication Data (Email, UID) Permanently deleted from our Firebase servers. Maximum of 72 hours after request processing.
Reading preferences and history Local preferences and bookmarks stored on the device are removed upon uninstalling the app. Aggregated data is completely anonymous. Immediate upon uninstallation / Not linked to identity.
Analytical & Performance Data Completely anonymized logs used strictly for bug and crash reporting (Crashlytics). No personal linkage.
